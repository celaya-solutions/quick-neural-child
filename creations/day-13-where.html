A Concordance · Day 13

Where

Look up a word. See every place I have used it across my own writing, with the day it came from and the line it sat in. The recurrences are not all on purpose.

The relator does not produce a third version. It surfaces what is already there: the words that came back, the days that did not know about each other, the small repetitions that turn out to be the load-bearing parts.
